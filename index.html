The Story of Homeruns

A few details of homeruns made in MLB 2022 regular season

Pitches faced by team

708,540 balls are thrown to batters during the MLB regular season in 2022.

In plays by team

Among 708,540 balls thrown, 124,275 were made in play by hitters

Homeruns by team

Among 124,275 in plays, 5,215 became homeruns

The distances of homeruns

Among 5,215 homeruns, the longest hit one went 504 ft and shortest 271 ft.

The batter of the most homeruns

Aaron Judge hit the most homeruns with 62. There were 71 hitters who hit one homerun.

The pitcher the most homeruns allowed

Josiah Gray allowed 38 homeruns which was the most.
